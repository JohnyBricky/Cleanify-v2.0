Company History

Cleanify was established in 2022 by four students from the University of Makati. They began providing services, when the pandemic struck. The team had no idea that these cleaning services would become popular and that many people would use them. Cleanify has been featured on several TV shows, vlogs, and newspapers. They've even offered their services to the palace, and Cleanify since then had been a private contractor for the Malacanang Palace's maintenance team.
Today, Cleanify is continuing to create a brand for itself, and many costumers are satisfied with the services they offer. Indeed, you contribute to the success of the CleanTeam. The team is led by John Bricks Ireneo, who is responsible for executive orders inside the company. He also oversees the manpower, technical, and advertising aspects of the commercial business.
